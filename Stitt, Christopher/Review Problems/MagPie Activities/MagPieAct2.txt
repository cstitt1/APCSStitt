MagPie Activity 2 exercises:

Prompt: My mother and I talked last night
Response: Tell me more about your family.
Prompt: I said no!
Response: Why so negative?
Prompt: The weather is nice
Response: Do you really think so?
Prompt: Do you know my brother?
Response: Why so negative?

When more than one keyword appears in the string it acts based on the last keyword searched for.
These responses can be prioritized by ordering which word it searches for first.

When a keyword is in another word, it works with that word as if it were alone. This causes
problems, like seeing the word "no" in "know".
