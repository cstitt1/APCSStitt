MagPie Activity 4 Exercises:

Start: Hello, let's talk.
Prompt: I want to build a robot.
Response: What would it mean to build a robot?
Prompt: I want to understand French.
Response: What would it mean to understand French?
Prompt: Do you like me?
Response: What makes you think that I like you?
Prompt: You confuse me
Response: What makes you think that I confuse you?

Possible Errors:
	"Want": "I want to be happy" causes "Would you really be happy if you had to be happy?"
		Fix: Check for a "to" after the "want".
	"I-you": Putting in the past tense of verb "v" would cause tense disagreement.
		Fix: Check for "-ed" at the end of the word.
